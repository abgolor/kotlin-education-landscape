# Kotlin Education Landscape Report (Summary)

## Overview

This research analyzes how Kotlin is taught across universities, bootcamps, and online platforms.

## Key Findings

- Online platforms are Kotlin-first
- Universities are slow to adopt Kotlin
- Bootcamps are bridging the gap

## Insight

Kotlin education is currently driven by industry platforms rather than academic institutions.
